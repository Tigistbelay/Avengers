Global Fashion in NYC, USA

The Importance of Global Fashion in NYC, USA apart from professional business attire, is wide-ranging from diverse cultural roots in clothing like: hats, boots, jeans, and leather jackets. Did you know that the lower east side neighborhood made New York a fashion capital? During the 20th century there was all types of aspects of garment making, from fabric to garment to marketing to stores on Fifth Avenue. The emigrated Asian, Hispanic, Irish, Italian, and Jewish migrated to New York throughout the 20th century who significantly influenced the culture of New York.
